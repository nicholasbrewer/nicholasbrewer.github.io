Nicholas Brewer, pianist, is a native of Tucson, Arizona where he began piano lessons at the age of five. During his 17 years of practice he has focused primarily on classical music, though he has a strong draw to jazz as well. For the past four years he has studied with Sar-Shalom Strong at Hamilton College. He has performed many times in University of Arizona's Centennial Hall and Hamilton College's Wellin Hall.
